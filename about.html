About Us

Welcome to Lenny's Mobile Detailing! We are a professional mobile car detailing service dedicated to providing top-notch detailing services at your convenience. Our team of experienced detailers is passionate about cars and committed to delivering exceptional results.

Founded in 2020, Lenny's Mobile Detailing has quickly become a trusted name in the industry. We use only the highest quality products and the latest techniques to ensure your vehicle looks its best. Whether you need a simple wash or a full detailing service, we've got you covered.

Our mission is to bring the shine to you, making it easy and convenient to keep your car in pristine condition. Thank you for choosing Lenny's Mobile Detailing. We look forward to serving you!
